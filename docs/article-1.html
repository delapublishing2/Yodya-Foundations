Thank You Welocalize

Published on October 13, 2024

Yodya would like to thank Welocalize Give Grant for their support in helping the Yodya foundation. With their generous donation of $500.00, Yodya is able to help support more women, children, families, and the elderly who are suffering, especially in disaster ravaged Haiti. We do this one hand at a time and cannot thank them enough for their support and reward.

Many innocent women, families, and children in the Dominican Republic are being kidnapped and held captive for ransom, leaving them with no choice but to surrender their belongings and even their lives. These victims are facing extreme hardship, with some having little to no food, and others forced to sleep on the streets.

The situation is dreadful, and it's essential that we act swiftly to provide assistance. Your donation can be a lifeline for families trapped in these horrific circumstances. Every dollar counts in helping these families escape their captors and rebuild their lives.

How your donation can help

Your contribution will go directly towards providing food, shelter, and safety for these victims and their families. It's our collective responsibility to help them break free from this ordeal and start anew.

Please lend a hand to those in need, and help save lives

By supporting this cause, you'll be lending a hand to a family in desperate need of rescue. Together, we can make a difference and bring hope back to those who have lost everything.
