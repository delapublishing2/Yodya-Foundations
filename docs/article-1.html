Thank You Welocalize

Published on November 4th, 2024

Yodya would like to thank Welocalize Give Grant for their support in helping the Yodya foundation. With their generous donation of $500.00, Yodya is able to help support more women, children, families, and the elderly who are suffering, especially in disaster ravaged Haiti. We do this one hand at a time and cannot thank them enough for their support and reward.

Your donation has definitely helped us come closer to our goals.

The President of Fondation Yodya, Economist Lyvens Guerrier, has greatly praised the generosity of WeLocalise for its outstanding philanthropy in donating to Fonyo.

However, we also want to bring attention to the harsh realities that Fonyo faces today. In Artibonite-Haiti, our loved ones have been brutally killed by armed gangs, leaving behind 70-80 victims and thousands more who were displaced and suffered unimaginable trauma.

7,000 Haitian citizens were forcibly deported and ransom demanded by Dominican immigration agents and police, with many being torn away from their families and babies. They were left to beg for help on the streets, bearing physical pain and suffering as they sought to survive.

We urge you to remember our prayers, donations, and support for those affected by this crisis. We cannot stress enough the urgency of addressing these injustices and providing aid to those who need it most
